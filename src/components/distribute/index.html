Frequently Asked Questions

  • The EpicF Games Store offers an international community of over 230 million users, an 88%/12% revenue split, and free services to help you market your game. For games developed on Unreal Engine, engine royalties do not apply to purchases made using EpicF's payment processing tools. In-game purchases and products using their own payment processing tools are subject to engine royalties.

  • To download games on EpicF Games, follow these steps:

    1. Open the EpicF Games Launcher on your computer.
    2. Log in to your EpicF Games account.
    3. Go to the Store tab and browse for the game you want to download.
    4. Click on the game to go to its detail page.
    5. Click the 'Get' or 'Purchase' button (if it's a free game, you'll see 'Get').
    6. Follow the prompts to complete the purchase or add the game to your library.
    7. Once the game is in your library, go to the Library tab.
    8. Find the game in your library and click the 'Install' button.
    9. Choose the installation location and click 'Install' again.
    10. Wait for the download and installation to complete.

    • There are more than 1000 different games at EpicFGames, including both paid and free games

  • Games and products purchased from the EpicF Games Store are typically eligible for refunds

    1. These eligible items are marked as 'refundable.' If a game or product is marked as 'non-refundable,' it means that particular item does not meet the criteria for a refunds
    2. Offers including virtual currency or other consumable goods marked as 'non-refundable' are also not eligible for refunds.
    3. Most in-app purchases are also non-refundable. EpicF cannot refund purchases made outside of the EpicF Games
